My Approach

How I Apply These Frameworks
These are not the only frameworks I draw from — they are simply the ones I return to most consistently. I apply a blend of these and other frameworks through a person-centric lens — always beginning with a deep understanding of learners as individuals: their prior knowledge, motivations, contexts, and constraints. No single model fits every situation, so I draw from this toolkit with intentionality and adaptability, choosing the right combination for the learner, the content, and the organizational environment.
In the development of learning experiences, I use tools such as Articulate Rise, Articulate Storyline, PlayPosit / WeVideo, Kaltura, and the organization's LMS to bring designs to life — always in service of the learning goals, never as ends in themselves.
